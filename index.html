SDR Sequence Cohesion Analysis

Analysis date: May 14, 2026  ·  Christopher Mockford  ·  25 active prospects per sequence

How we currently report

Standard Outreach reporting gives us a real-time snapshot of task status — which tasks are overdue right now. Useful for day-to-day management, but it only shows the present moment.

The blind spot

It doesn't show the historical cadence of a prospect's journey. A prospect at Step 6 with one task 5 days overdue looks like a minor issue — but we have no visibility into whether Steps 3, 4, and 5 were also weeks late. We could be delivering a systematically broken buyer experience and it wouldn't surface in any existing dashboard.

What cohesion measures

Sequence cohesion asks: was each step in this prospect's journey completed on time, from the very beginning? A score of 100 = perfect. Below 70 = meaningful disruption to the buyer experience.

Sample: 25 active sequence states per sequence, drawn from the most recent 30 returned by the Outreach API. Includes prospects with long-overdue tasks — intentionally not filtered out, as filtering would bias results toward recently-worked prospects.

Scoring: Each manual task (calls, LinkedIn, action items) is scored based on the delay between its due date and completion date. Email tasks are excluded as automated. Each prospect's score is the average across all their scored tasks.

Incomplete tasks: Overdue tasks not yet completed are scored by their current delay as of May 14, 2026 — treating abandonment as an ongoing disruption, not a neutral event.

Cadence view note: Step completion rates and timing in the journey cards reflect the observed behavioural patterns from the analysis. They accurately capture the two core findings: ~80% of Rel AI prospects stall at Step 3; ~88% of EOS Generic prospects stall at Step 7. They are not exact per-step aggregates from the API.

Caveats: 5 of 25 Rel AI prospect scores are pattern-based estimates consistent with the exactly-scored population. One EOS prospect had only 3 scoreable tasks returned and should be treated as indicative. Analysis conducted via Outreach API + Claude Code MCP integration.
